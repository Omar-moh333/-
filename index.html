About Kidney Failure

Kidney failure happens when kidneys can't filter waste. It may be acute or chronic.

Symptoms

  • Fatigue
  • Swelling
  • Nausea
  • Shortness of breath
  • Confusion
  • Low urine

Treatment

Dialysis, medication, or transplant. Early detection is key.
