About Kidney Failure

Failure happens when kidneys can't filter waste. May be acute or chronic.

  • Fatigue
  • Swelling
  • Nausea
  • Shortness of breath
  • Confusion
  • Low urine

Treatment includes dialysis, medication, or transplant.
