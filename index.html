About Kidney Failure

Kidney failure occurs when the kidneys lose the ability to filter waste from the blood. It can be acute or chronic.

Symptoms

  • Fatigue
  • Swelling in legs and ankles
  • Nausea
  • Shortness of breath
  • Confusion
  • Reduced urine output

Treatment

Treatments include dialysis, medications, and kidney transplant.
